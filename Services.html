Top-quality auto repair at Thros Auto Service in St. Charles, MO.

Our Services

At Thros Auto Service in St. Charles, Missouri, we’re proud to offer top-notch auto repair services to our customers in St. Charles, St. Peters, and Orchard Farm. Our experienced mechanics have the skills and expertise to handle all of your car repair needs, from routine maintenance to major repairs. Whether you need an oil change, brake service, or engine repair, we’ve got you covered. Trust Thros Auto Service for all of your auto repair needs in the St. Charles area.

Vehicle Maintenance and Fleet Services

Keep your vehicles running smoothly with our Vehicle Maintenance and Fleet Services. Our experienced mechanics provide top-quality maintenance and repair services to keep your fleet on the road. Trust us for all of your vehicle maintenance needs.

Safety and Emissions Tests

Ensure your vehicle meets safety and emissions standards with our comprehensive testing services. Our experienced technicians will thoroughly inspect your vehicle and provide any necessary repairs to keep you on the road safely and legally.

Tires and Alignments

Keep your vehicle running smoothly with our tire and alignment services. From tire rotations and replacements to wheel alignments and balancing, our skilled mechanics will ensure your car handles perfectly on the road

Brakes

Stay safe on the road with our brake repair and replacement services. Our expert mechanics will inspect your brakes and provide any necessary repairs or replacements to keep you stopping safely and reliably.

Heating and Cooling Systems

Stay comfortable in your car with our heating and cooling system services. Our experienced technicians will diagnose and repair any issues with your vehicle’s HVAC system to keep you cool in the summer and warm in the winter.

Engine, Transmission Replacement

Get back on the road quickly with our engine and transmission replacement services. Our skilled mechanics can replace your vehicle’s engine or transmission with a high-quality new or rebuilt unit to restore its performance.

Computer Diagnostics

Diagnose and fix complex issues with our computer diagnostic services. Our advanced diagnostic equipment can quickly identify problems with your vehicle, allowing our experienced technicians to provide fast and accurate repairs.

Electrical Repair

Keep your car’s electrical systems running smoothly with our electrical repair services. From battery replacements to wiring repairs, our skilled mechanics have the expertise to fix any electrical issue your vehicle may have.

Electric Charging Stations

Charge up your electric vehicle at our convenient charging stations. Our fast and reliable charging equipment will get you back on the road quickly and easily.
